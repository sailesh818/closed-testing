Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

Closed Testing (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application, Closed Testing (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to support the app’s core functionality:

Account Information:
When you register or log in, we collect your email address and password through Firebase Authentication.
Passwords are securely handled by Firebase and are never visible to us.

App Submission Data:
Users may submit app information for closed testing, such as:

  • Google Group link
  • Android beta/testing link
  • Website or related links

This information is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and may be shown to other users for testing purposes.

Usage Data:
We may collect anonymous technical information such as crash reports, device type, and in-app behavior through Firebase services to improve app performance. This information does not include personally identifiable data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Authenticate user accounts
  • Allow users to register and share apps for closed testing
  • Enable users to test apps submitted by others
  • Improve app performance and user experience

We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising or marketing.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share information in the following cases:

Service Providers (Firebase by Google):
Firebase handles authentication, data storage, and analytics according to Google’s Privacy Policy.

Legal Requirements:
We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

We do not share your information with unauthorized third parties.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including HTTPS encryption and Firebase Security Rules. Although no online system is completely secure, we take strong measures to protect your data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate the App. If you wish to delete your account and all related data, you may contact us at:

Email: appcoding15@gmail.com

We will process your request within a reasonable time.

6. Children’s Privacy

Closed Testing is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, please contact us at:

Email: appcoding15@gmail.com
